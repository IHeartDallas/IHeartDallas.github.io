Online Tech Education

Over the past two years I set out to enhance my web and software development skills. Even though I am a tech writer and am not new to the software industry, this was and is still is a challenging journey. While I completed a qaulity online program, it only piqued my interest to learn more. I felt as though so many concepts and gaps were missing in this learning experience. Like many other self-taught developers, you run into many road blocks and finally hit the wall -- Where do I go next!


Since technology drives our world, I strongly believe there is a need for qaulity online tech offerings. Consequently, I would like to share my experience with you in the event that you might have a client that is in this industry. I would be glad if you could share this critique. Thank you kindly for your time and consideration.
